Micro Poultry Farm Project

Zakhelilakhi (Pty) Ltd raises broiler chicks in 6-week cycles for sales to butcheries, retail shops, events, and local communities. Chicks are purchased from credible suppliers and raised at our farm in Sinqobile, Mpumalanga. Health and safety standards are maintained strictly to ensure quality.

Our model ensures cost-effective operations and profitability, with disinfection between each cycle and reinvestment into growing our infrastructure.
